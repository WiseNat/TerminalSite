For my Computer Science A-Level coursework, I created a remote-controlled camera.

An Android app allowed you to control the camera's pitch, yaw, and shutter, with a real-time video feed.
It also hosted face detection to automatically centre the camera on a person's face.
Once a picture had been taken, it would automatically be sent to the user's phone and stored.

This was my first exposure to RPIs, 3D printing, Kotlin, Android App Development, and Flask.
